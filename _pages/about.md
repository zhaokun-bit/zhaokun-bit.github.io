I am a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), co-advised by Prof. Junqiang Xi and Prof. Wenshuo Wang. Currently, I am a visiting scholar at the School of Computing and Data Science (CCDS), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, working under the supervision of Prof. Erik Cambria. Prior to my Ph.D. journey, I received my Bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2022.

My research interests include intelligent driving/cockpits, human-robot interaction, driving style analysis, and multimodal Large Language Models (LLMs).
